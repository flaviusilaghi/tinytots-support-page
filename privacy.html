Privacy Policy for Tiny Tots - Baby Tracker Log

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TinyTots ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Tiny Tots - Baby Tracker Log (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our service to you.

a) Information You Provide to Us:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address.
  • Child's Information: You provide information about your child, including their name, date of birth, and sex. This information is essential for the App's tracking features.
  • Log Data: You provide data about your child's activities, such as feeding sessions (type, duration, amount), sleep sessions, diaper changes, pumping sessions, and growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference).

b) Information We Collect Automatically:

We do not directly collect information automatically. However, our third-party service providers (Firebase and RevenueCat) may collect certain information automatically as described in their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the App.
  • To create personalized charts, statistics, and timelines for you.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices.
  • To manage your account, including processing subscriptions and sending password reset emails.
  • To communicate with you about your account or updates to our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our App:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for user authentication, database storage (Firestore), and application performance monitoring. Firebase may collect identifiers for analytics and usage data. You can learn more about how Firebase handles data in Google's Privacy & Terms.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage and process your in-app subscriptions and trials. RevenueCat may collect and process transaction data and device identifiers to validate purchases. You can view RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more details.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties other than these trusted service providers.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information by leveraging secure cloud infrastructure provided by Firebase. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at tinytotsapp@gmail.com. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove your data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for use by parents and legal guardians to track information about their children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. All information about children is provided with the consent of the parent or guardian using the App. If you become aware that a child has provided us with Personal Information without parental consent, please contact us.

7. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
